About Online Quran Academy

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality Quran education through live interactive online classes. We believe in nurturing students with love, knowledge, and qualified tutors to master the Quran with Tajweed, Hifz, Tafseer, and more.

Established in 2020, our academy has helped hundreds of students worldwide improve their Quranic knowledge in an engaging and supportive environment.
